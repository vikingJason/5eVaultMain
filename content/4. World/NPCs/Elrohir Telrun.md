> [!info|bg-c-purple] Overview
> Elrohir Telrun is a stalwart Leonin serving as the Sheriff of Petalwood. Known for his unwavering dedication to law and order, he is both respected and feared by the townspeople. Beneath his noble exterior lies a hidden allegiance to The Dawn's Shadow, a covert branch of the King's Guard dedicated to rooting out corruption and protecting the kingdom from unseen threats.

## Traits

> [!recite|txt-Center] **Physical Description**
> Elrohir stands at an imposing 6'5" with a muscular build covered in tawny fur. His mane is a darker shade of brown, braided in places to signify his status and achievements. His golden eyes have an intense, piercing gaze, and he carries a large, ornate greatsword with intricate designs that reflect his noble heritage.

> [!recite|title-Centre] **Mannerisms**
> Elrohir speaks with a deep, commanding voice that brooks no argument. He has a habit of tapping his fingers on the hilt of his sword when deep in thought. His movements are calculated and deliberate, reflecting his disciplined nature.
>
>> [!quote] ***"In Petalwood, the law is not just a guideline—it is the foundation of our lives. Abide by it, or face the consequences."***

> [!recite|title-Centre] **Abilities**
> Elrohir is a formidable warrior with exceptional strength and combat skills. His training with The Dawn's Shadow has also granted him proficiency in stealth and subterfuge. He can track targets with uncanny precision and is an expert in hand-to-hand combat as well as swordsmanship.



<br>

## Personality


> [!recite|title-Center] **World View**
> Elrohir believes that law and order are paramount to maintaining peace and stability. He sees the world in shades of black and white, with little room for moral ambiguity. However, his secret role in The Dawn's Shadow allows him to bend the rules when necessary to serve the greater good.
> 
> > [!warning]
> > ***Players who show respect for the law and justice will find Elrohir approachable (DC 10 for social interactions). Those who engage in criminal activities or question his methods will find him unyielding (DC 20).***

> [!recite|title-Centre] **Motivations**
> Elrohir is motivated by a desire to protect Petalwood and uphold the law. His loyalty to the King's Guard and The Dawn's Shadow drives him to root out corruption and safeguard the kingdom from hidden threats.

> [!recite|title-Centre] **Ideals**
> **Justice must be served, no matter the cost.**

> [!recite|title-Centre] **Bonds**
> Elrohir is deeply loyal to the King's Guard and The Dawn's Shadow. He also has a strong bond with the townspeople of Petalwood, whom he views as his responsibility to protect.

> [!recite|title-Centre] **Flaw**
> His rigid adherence to the law can make him inflexible and harsh. He struggles with the moral complexities of his secret role in The Dawn's Shadow.

> [!recite|title-Centre] **Secrets**
> Elrohir's membership in The Dawn's Shadow is known only to a select few. He is tasked with gathering intelligence on corrupt officials within Petalwood.

> [!recite|title-Centre] **Goals**
> To maintain order in Petalwood and uncover any threats to the kingdom. Elrohir also seeks to balance his duties as sheriff with his covert operations for The Dawn's Shadow.

> [!recite|title-Centre] **Likes/Dislikes**
> ***Likes:*** Discipline, justice, loyalty, strategic planning.
>
> ***Dislikes:*** Corruption, chaos, dishonesty, inefficiency.

<br>

## Relationships

> [!kith|family] **Family** 
> Elrohir's family resides in the Leonin homeland. He maintains regular contact with them and seeks their counsel on important matters.
> 

> [!kith|friend]  **Friends** 
> Elrohir has a few trusted friends within the King's Guard and The Dawn's Shadow. He also shares a close friendship with the town's healer, an elf named Arannis.
> 

> [!kith|romantic]  **Romantic**
> Elrohir is romantically involved with a human woman named Elara, who runs a local inn. Their relationship is kept secret due to his covert operations.
> 

> [!kith|antagonist]  **Rivals** 
> Elrohir has a rival in a local merchant named Varis, who he suspects of illegal activities. Their interactions are tense and filled with underlying hostility.


<br>

> [!timeline|title-Centre] **Notable History**
> - **15 years ago**: Elrohir joined the King's Guard and quickly rose through the ranks.
> - **10 years ago**: He was recruited into The Dawn's Shadow for his exceptional skills and loyalty.
> - **5 years ago**: Elrohir was assigned as the Sheriff of Petalwood to maintain order and gather intelligence.
> - **Present**: He continues to serve as sheriff while secretly working to uncover corruption within Petalwood.

<br>

> [!example] **Inventory**
> - Ornate greatsword with magical enhancements.
> - A badge of office as Sheriff of Petalwood.
> - Cloak of Elvenkind, aiding in stealth.
> - Ring of Mind Shielding, protecting against mental intrusion.

> [!warning] **Potential Quest hook**
> The party is approached by Elrohir to assist in a covert operation to expose and dismantle a corrupt network within Petalwood. In return, he offers his protection and access to valuable resources.
