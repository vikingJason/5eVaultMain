# Charm of Animal Conjuring

This charm allows you to cast the [conjure animals](2-Mechanics/CLI/spells/conjure-animals.md) spell (3rd-level version) as an action. Once used three times, the charm vanishes from you.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 228*
