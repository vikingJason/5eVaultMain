# Sui
*Source: Tome of Heroes p. 261*  

*The rune of self*

**Type**: Range Rune

**Representation**: Self

**Spellcasting Focus**: When you use *sui* as a spellcasting focus while casting a spell with a range of self, you don't need to use somatic or verbal components to cast the spell.
