# Deadly Bite
*Source: Flee, Mortals! p. 44*  

**Prerequisite**: Lion, tiger, or jaguar

When the wildcat hits a [prone](2-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#prone) creature with a Bite attack, the attack is a critical hit.
