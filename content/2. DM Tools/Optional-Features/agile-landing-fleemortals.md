# Agile Landing
*Source: Flee, Mortals! p. 44*  

**Prerequisite**: Any Medium wildcat

The wildcat takes no damage from falling 40 feet or less, provided they aren't [incapacitated](2-Mechanics/CLI/rules/conditions.md#incapacitated).
